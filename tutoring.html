    How to prepare for 1-on-1 tutoring

    When preparing for a tutoring session, you should have a clear goal in mind. For instance, if you are unsure about for-loops in Java, you should let me know that you know so far about them and what your pain points are. If you're unsure about what you do or don't know on a specific subject, share what you think you know! This will help me to narrow down the issue and help you to hammer out the problem going forward. I will also gladly make example problems for us to go over!
